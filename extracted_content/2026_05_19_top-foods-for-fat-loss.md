## Top Foods That Support Healthy Fat Loss


Including nutrient-rich foods in your daily diet can improve fullness, nutritional quality, and overall wellness. Indian meals naturally offer many wholesome options that support sustainable fat management.


### 1. Oats


Oats are rich in soluble fiber that helps improve fullness and support healthy digestion. They make an excellent breakfast option for weight management.


### 2. Eggs


Eggs provide high-quality protein that helps maintain muscle mass and reduce unnecessary hunger during the day.


### 3. Paneer


Paneer is a popular Indian protein source that supports satiety and helps maintain balanced nutrition during fat loss.


### 4. Green Vegetables


Leafy vegetables are low in calories but high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals that support overall health.


### 5. Greek Yogurt and Curd


Curd and yogurt provide protein and probiotics that support digestion and improve gut health.


### 6. Fruits


Seasonal fruits provide natural sweetness, antioxidants, hydration, and fiber while supporting healthy eating habits.


### 7. Lentils and Dal


Indian dals are excellent protein and fiber sources that help maintain fullness and balanced energy.


### 8. Nuts and Seeds


Healthy fats from almonds, walnuts, chia seeds, and flaxseeds support satiety and nutritional balance when consumed in moderation.
