## How to Lose 8 Kg in 1 Month Naturally


To lose 8 kg in 1 month safely, it is important to maintain a calorie deficit while ensuring the body receives proper nutrition. Extreme starvation diets may lead to muscle loss, weakness, and nutritional deficiencies.

- Eat high-protein meals
- Avoid sugary beverages
- Increase daily physical activity
- Walk at least 8,000–10,000 steps daily
- Sleep 7–8 hours regularly
- Drink sufficient water
- Reduce processed food intake

## Hydration and Sleep for Faster Fat Loss


Hydration supports digestion and metabolism. Drinking enough water may help reduce overeating and improve energy levels. Sleep is equally important because poor sleep may increase hunger hormones and cravings.


Aim for at least 2.5–3 liters of water daily and maintain a consistent sleep routine for better fat-burning support.
