## Best Diet Plan for Weight Gain


A structured diet plays a crucial role in weight gain. The goal is to consume nutrient-dense calories rather than empty calories.


### 1. Increase Calorie Intake


You need to consume more calories than your body burns. However, these calories should come from healthy sources like whole grains, nuts, seeds, and dairy products.


### 2. Focus on Protein-Rich Foods


Protein helps in muscle building. Include foods such as:

- Eggs
- Paneer
- Chicken
- Lentils
- Milk
- Protein shakes



### 3. Healthy Carbohydrates


Carbohydrates provide energy and support weight gain. Choose complex carbs like brown rice, oats, quinoa, and whole wheat products.


### 4. Healthy Fats


Include healthy fats such as:

- Avocados
- Almonds
- Walnuts
- Olive oil
- Peanut butter



### 5. Frequent Meals


Eat 5–6 small meals throughout the day instead of 2–3 large meals to maintain consistent calorie intake.
