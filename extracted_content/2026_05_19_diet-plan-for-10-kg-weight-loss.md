## Important Principles of a 10 KG Weight Loss Diet Plan


An effective weight loss plan should focus on improving overall nutritional quality rather than eliminating entire food groups. The right combination of proteins, complex carbohydrates, healthy fats, and fiber helps maintain satiety and stable energy levels.


### Balanced Nutrition


Meals should include adequate protein, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats to support metabolism and muscle health.


### Portion Control


Eating mindful portions helps create a calorie deficit naturally without making the body feel deprived.


### Hydration


Drinking enough water supports digestion, appetite regulation, and overall metabolic functioning.


### Regular Meal Timing


Skipping meals may increase cravings and overeating later in the day. Consistent meal timing improves energy balance.


### Physical Activity


Combining healthy nutrition with walking, strength training, yoga, or cardio exercises improves fat-burning efficiency.
