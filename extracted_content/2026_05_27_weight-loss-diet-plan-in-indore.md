## Best Foods for Weight Loss


### High-Protein Foods


Protein-rich foods such as eggs, paneer, sprouts, lentils, tofu, and Greek yogurt help maintain fullness and reduce overeating.


### Fiber-Rich Vegetables


Vegetables like spinach, cucumber, broccoli, bottle gourd, and carrots support digestion and improve satiety.


### Healthy Hydration


Drinking enough water supports metabolism and helps control unnecessary hunger cravings.


### Foods to Avoid


Reduce sugary beverages, bakery items, fried snacks, processed foods, and excessive junk food for better results.

Healthy habits combined with proper nutrition improve consistency and long-term fat-loss success.
