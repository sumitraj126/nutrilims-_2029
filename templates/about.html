Oshin Ambekar

Founder & Head Nutritionist

Hello, I am Oshin Ambekar. My mission is to guide you towards a healthier, more balanced life through science-backed, sustainable nutrition. I believe that food is medicine, and eating healthy should be a source of joy, not a stressful math equation of calories.

With over 8 years of experience in clinical dietetics and metabolic health, I have helped more than 500 clients reverse PCOS, manage Diabetes, lose weight sustainably, and build a lasting relationship with food.

My consulting philosophy focuses on the "Indian Home Kitchen". I do not recommend expensive, exotic ingredients. Instead, I help you optimize local, seasonal foods that are already in your pantry. By combining clinical biochemistry with practical lifestyle adjustments, we create plans that truly fit your daily routine.
